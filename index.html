Experience management is inherently about transformation and change
We constantly change on our path to become an XM professional and aspire to transform to be our bests as we learn and practice throughout our careers.
This interview series tries to show personal journeys of these professionals and showcase how their experience brings new dimensions and insights to our field.

Ákos Tolnai
About me
I am a certified customer experience professional. I created an irrationality-based innovation framework back in 2009. It uses customer experience expectations and pattern recognition to map out potential hits in the innovation portfolio.
Together with my partner and our team at AbilityMatrix, we help innovative companies with go-to market strategies and innovation consulting. Experience management is my passion. I believe that the critical component of almost every breakthrough innovation is changing the customer experience. For the last couple years, I’ve been obsessed with organizational design and behavioral design.
Have any Questions? (FAQ)
Who is Discover XM for?
Discover XM is for anyone who is interested in experience management. We would like to move beyond professional experience and lessons learned and show the people behind the profession. If you plan to hire your next consultant, colleague, or speaker, maybe you can find not only the best professionals but also the people you would want to spend time with.
How can I be a part of Discover XM?
If you want to work on this project, please feel free to use the contact form and use your Linkedin profile. We'll get in touch with you as soon as we have an opening.
How can I contact you?
We have a plan on how to proceed but feel free to contact us with your story and we will get back to you as soon as we can. Please keep in mind that this a side-project for us at AbilityMatrix, so it might not happen in 2 hours. Our way to thank the community.
Does it cost anything to watch the interviews?
Everything is free and we do not charge anybody or take money from anyone. We try to be fully independent and no sponsor money can buy you an interview with us. Our goal is to show people independent of their brands and focus on their personal journey that was before they got involved with XM and will continue even if XM will not exist in 20 years.
